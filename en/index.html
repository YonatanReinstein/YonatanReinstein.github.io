About the Clinic

At our clinic, you will enjoy advanced dental treatments in a relaxed atmosphere, with personal attention and an emphasis on patient comfort. We specialize in conservative treatments, rehabilitation, surgery, and implants using modern equipment to improve your treatment experience and smile aesthetics.

About Dr. Benjamin Reinstein:

  • Dr. Reinstein holds a DMD degree from the Faculty of Dental Medicine of the Hebrew University and Hadassah Ein Kerem.
  • Graduated from advanced training in oral rehabilitation, complex root canal therapy, and surgical extractions at Sheba Medical Center.
  • Member of the Israeli Association of Dental Implants.
  • Recipient of the IDF Medical Services Excellence Award for managing military dental clinics.
